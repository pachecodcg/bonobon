The Pistachio Wave

Arcor's Bon o Bon has been an Argentine icon since 1984: a crispy wafer sphere filled with cream and coated in chocolate. Produced at a rate of 10,000 units per minute, it reaches over 80 countries and is the second best selling bonbon on the planet.

The Pistacho edition rides the global pistachio craze. Launched in Brazil, it pairs a creamy pistachio filling with a crunchy wafer shell and a double coating of dark chocolate (53% cacao). It sold out within hours in Argentine kioscos that managed to import it.
